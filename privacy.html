Le Sync

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 June 2026

Le Sync is a one-way bridge that copies your health data from Google Health into Apple Health on your iPhone. This policy explains exactly what the app accesses and what it does with it. In short: your health data stays on your device. We do not run servers that receive it, and we do not track you.

What the app accesses

With your permission, Le Sync reads the following from your Google Health account using read-only access:

  • Activity and fitness: steps, distance, active energy
  • Heart and respiratory metrics: heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability, blood oxygen, respiratory rate
  • Sleep: sleep sessions and sleep stages

Le Sync only ever requests read-only Google authorization. It cannot change, add to, or delete anything in your Google account.

How the app uses it

Le Sync writes the data it reads into Apple Health on your device, sorted into the matching Apple Health categories. Moving your data into Apple Health is the app's only purpose.

All processing happens on your iPhone. Your health data is never sent to, or stored on, any server operated by us. Le Sync has no backend, no accounts, and no database.

On-device only

  • No health data leaves your device, except the direct, encrypted connection between your iPhone and Google's API used to fetch your own data.
  • We do not store your health data anywhere off your device.
  • Le Sync never stores your health data in iCloud, and never uses it for advertising or data-mining (consistent with Apple App Store Review Guideline 5.1.3).

Analytics, tracking, and advertising

None. Le Sync contains no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. We do not build a profile of you, and we do not share data with data brokers.

Sign-in tokens

To connect to Google Health, the app uses Google Sign-In. The resulting authorization tokens are stored only in the iOS Keychain, managed by Google's Sign-In SDK. We never see, store, or transmit these tokens ourselves.

Third parties

  • Google Sign-In and the Google Health API — used solely to authorize the app and fetch your own health data, at your request. Your use of Google is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Le Sync uses no other third-party services to collect or process your data.

Data retention and deletion

  • We retain nothing, because we never receive your data.
  • The data Le Sync writes into Apple Health lives in Apple Health on your device, under your control. To delete it, remove the samples in the Apple Health app, or delete Le Sync (and, if you wish, remove its data in Apple Health).
  • Signing out clears the app's stored Google authorization tokens. Deleting the app removes its on-device settings and cached sync checkpoints; you will need to sign in again if you reinstall.

How to revoke consent

  • In Le Sync, sign out (Settings → Disconnect & Reset). This revokes the app's Google authorization and clears its local sync state.
  • You can also review or revoke Le Sync's access at any time from your Google Account permissions page (myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party access).
  • You control Apple Health write permissions in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

Children

Le Sync is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised version at the same location.
